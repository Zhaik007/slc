We are Students of 25th Group at NUFYP (CPS). We are delighted to see you on our website. Here we will share our activities and reports about what we have achieved so far

Let's briefly Introduce every member of our team:

  • Zhassulan Toleubekov - is a eco activist, volunteer and just a chill guy who enjoy helping people and contributing to the prosperity of our community. He was active member of school eco club(deputy minister), volunteer of Recycle birge (2021-2024), Airvision Ambassador (2023-2024), UNICEF volunteers4Climate programmes coordinator of the volunteers

    Among his achievements are several awards, such as The best leader of Youth movement (Uly dala saryny), and «Eco-initiative of the Year» among young leaders for the BRICS and SCO Young Leaders Award. Currently he is developing project dedicated to the same purpose - raise awareness among young people about environmental problems. He is an active member of NU Green Society and SDSN Youth Kazakhstan Environmental Department
  • Gulden Sagymbayeva - is a very disciplined student, who is planning to study at ___ major

Our team is committed to raising community awareness about Responsible consumption, especially focusing on personal responsibility of every student of NU, as we chose the topic of our social challenge as recycling

Our team encourages every member of our community to preserve natural resources, that are present in limited amount in our planet

Excessive use of natural resources and the extraction of fossil fuels, needed to sustain our daily needs has significant environmental consequences, contributing to land, water,soil and air pollution

Let's dive into the more consience world and do as much we can to keep our planet clean!
