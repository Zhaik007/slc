Sll bootcamp 20th February (2025)

Finding opportunities

View this post on Instagram

On February 20th, the Bootcamp of the Sustainability Living Lab program was held at Nazarbayev University, organized by the Office of Sustainable Development in partnership with Chevron. The event was attended by students from NU, SKU named after Kozybaiyev, KarU named after Buketov, and Yessenov University. Participants presented the current progress of their projects and received valuable advice from invited experts

Speakers, including researchers from ACBK, representatives from UNDP, and successful eco-entrepreneurs, shared their experiences and knowledge, inspiring students to continue their work. The event concluded with a Startup Accelerator Workshop, conducted by business trainer ABC, where participants gained practical skills for developing and scaling their environmentally sustainable startups

Our SLC team also attended this event and made useful connection with the presenters. At the event we learned about different innovative aproaches that could reduce the negative impact on environment and we alongside Ainur from OSD NU, discussed possibility of developing a map of separate waste collection on the campus territory
